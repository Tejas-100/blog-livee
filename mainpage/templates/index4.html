MAKE ONE Crore Rupees IN ONE Month

Is this a joke or is it?

January 3, 2020 Shravni

I read a story about a college student who came up with an idea to help him pay for college. This was back in the earlier days of the internet. He put up a simple website which was a single page that was one big graphic. He then offered to sell 'real estate' in the form of pixels. Advertisers would purchase space at ₹70 per pixel, and submit small graphical ads a number of pixels wide by high. This was one of the earlier 'memes' and it had attracted enough attention that he was actually able to generate demand. He ended up selling 1 lakh pixels, and earning ₹1 crore. Thanks to this clever idea. I think it was called 'the crore rupees home page' or something like that. (you can google it).

Another example - Nowadays, a simple app can net you a lot of rupees. Another example I can think of is the modern version of 'the pet rock' in the form of a guy who made an 'app' marketed as a ghost finder. It claimed that it used the sensors of the phone to identify spirit activity. A fun and easy to create app that i think sold for ₹1 and netted worth of a crores sales.

Now obviously these ideas are spent, but the key is to adopt a mind-set that can identify and develop similar ideas. These are obviously just non-concrete 'notions', but if they trigger a new way of thinking for you then just about anything's possible. 30 days is a bit tougher. That would depend on whatever scheme you come up with.

So use these examples as inspiration - good luck!
